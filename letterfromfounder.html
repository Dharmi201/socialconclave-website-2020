Letter from Founder

Dear Delegates,
NMIMS‘ MPSTME was established with the aim to offer its students a world-class learning experience. In a college filled with technically inclined students, Students’ Council’s Social Impact was started in 2010 with a vision to create a platform for the students of MPSTME to actively engage in social issues that concern the society as a whole. Through the years Social Impact has conducted donation drives, volunteering programs, and various other campaigns, aiming for the betterment of the society.
The second edition of Social Conclave gives us an opportunity, yet another year, to bring together like-minded and socially conscious people from across the nation to address and discuss various socio-economic concerns of the nation and strive to find feasible solutions with everlasting impacts, because we believe in impacting lives, at every step of our journey.
What makes Social Conclave different from any other conference is that on the second day we will be organising special field trips to get a better understanding of the issues undertaken and the solutions discussed upon and decided at the end of the three days, will be implemented by Social Impact throughout the following year. The winning solutions of Social Conclave 2018 are done with the framework at the ground level and are under implementation already!
Social Conclave 2019, which is a three-day long conference will be hosting distinguished speakers and panellists who will guide the delegates to come up with a feasible solution of their particular agenda. We strive towards making this a joyful experience by holding various social events to enhance the bonding between the delegates and the organising committee, giving it a true feel of a social gathering.
With Social Conclave, we aim at empowering the students who have regard and sensitivity for the society and are determined to bring about a change in the society fraught with corruption and vices, by not only putting forward their propositions but also working towards their execution.
We sincerely hope you will join us in this one of a kind conference. Please feel free to contact me or my team regarding any queries or concerns on the links provided. We look forward to having you with us!

Sarthak Monga

Founder and President,
