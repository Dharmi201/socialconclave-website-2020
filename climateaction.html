Climate Action

Global warming, Pollution and Climate change are commonly used terms these days with the rapidly changing climate. The global average atmospheric carbon dioxide in 2018 was 407.4 parts per million (ppm for short), with a range of uncertainty of plus or minus 0.1 ppm. Carbon dioxide levelstoday are higher than at any point in at least the past 800,000 years.
Denying climate change is like inviting death. Human activities are the leading cause of all the catastrophic events happening in our planet. Neglecting the necessary precautions of using our mother nature needs to stop.
We have very little time left to save our planet from getting destroyed. Planting trees, Conserving wildlife and Population Control are a few ways of saving our planet.
