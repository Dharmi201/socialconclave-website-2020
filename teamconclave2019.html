Team Conclave 2019

We at Conclave believe in doing our bit to bring about a big change one problem at a time

Founder and President:

Sarthak
Monga

The father of Social Conclave and the current President of Social Conclave 2019, Sarthak Monga, is an all-rounder in its true senses, with him being a consistent University Topper, NMIMS University’s President, General Secretary of NMIMS’s MPSTME, founder of MPSTME’s Students’ Council’s Research Cell, having conducted the best year till now of MPSTME’s Students’ Council’s Social Impact as its Secretary in 2017-18 and moreover, having a heart of gold and a genius brain. Social Conclave was a result of his undeterred efforts and determination and he has proven it time and again that a leader like him does not just lead the way, but takes everyone hand in hand, to greater heights.

Chairperson:

Nitin
Jain

A multitasker and a dedicated leader, Nitin Jain is the guardian angel of Social Conclave. Currently the Secretary of Students’ Council’s Social Impact for 2018-19 and the Vice Chairperson of Sattva 2019- MPSTME’s annual cultural fest, Nitin has seen Social Conclave nurture from a mere idea to a remarkable conference in real time. His ideology and knowledge about each area of work has always made him the pushing force of Social Concave since its birth and now as the Chairperson of this conference, we can only imagine barriers being broken.

Vice-Chairperson:

Yash
Joshi

Yash Joshi’s credentials make him a leader you can learn a lot from on a professional as well as a personal level. He, at the young age of 19 became the youngest panellist in history to have appeared on a prime-time debate on Republic TV, headed by the famous Arnab Goswami. An official writer at The Frustrated Indian page which has over 1 million followers, he has bestowed his writing skills upon us by formulating a Social Media and Content Writing department for the first time in college, in 2018 as part of Social Conclave 2018 and displayed his exceptional skills for newbies to learn from.

Vice-President:

Shilpika
Agarwal

A genius and a workaholic, Shilpika has done some indelible work in the past as a young woman with a goal of reaching heights and inspiring one and all. The first Secretary of MPSTME’s Students’ Council’s Research Cell, she collaborated with Google for her first event, making an impact throughout the campus with her display of dedication and creative mindset. Guiding a committee in its formative years is one of the most crucial tasks, and she sure is doing it right with her extraordinary leadership qualities.

Vice-President:

Shivani
Sharma

To have made the nation proud at NASA by bringing the Educational Management award home with her team, is an accomplishment that Shivani Sharma achieved with the pure use of her intellect. Sunshine for all of us, Shivani, an integral part of Social Conclave, has always been the timeless support that this conference always needed and is proud to have it. The current Secretary of MPSTME’s Students’ Council’s Alumni Association, she became the first Secretary to have organised an alumni meet successfully in the history of MPSTME, as an ode to MPSTME’s distinguished alumni.
